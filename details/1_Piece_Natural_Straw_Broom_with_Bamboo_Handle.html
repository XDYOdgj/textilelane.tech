Traditional Thai Natural Straw Broom

This handcrafted broom is made from meticulously selected Thai mountain grass, offering a rich, natural texture for effective cleaning. Paired with a sturdy bamboo handle, it combines strength and flexibility, making it perfect for sweeping across diverse surfaces like wood, tile, and even outdoor areas. The traditional design adds a warm, authentic touch to your home, while the durable construction ensures it lasts for years.

Features

  • 100% natural materials from Thailand for eco-friendly and sustainable cleaning
  • Handmade construction with reinforced bamboo handle for long-term use
  • Lightweight and comfortable design for easy, effortless sweeping

  • Includes a traditional loincloth storage bag for keeping your broom clean and organized

Why Choose This Broom?

Whether you're cleaning your kitchen, bathroom, or even your backyard, this versatile broom is built to handle it all.
