iHUFeather Rainbow Static Duster

Clean smarter with this innovative static duster that keeps your spaces dust-free without the mess.

Effortless Cleaning for Every Surface

Designed with a 24-inch length and a sturdy handle, the iHUFeather Rainbow Static Duster gives you the reach and control needed for thorough cleaning. Perfect for tackling furniture, car dashboards, and office setups, it is built to make your cleaning routine easier and more effective.

Static Electricity Meets Microfiber Innovation

Thanks to its static electricity feature, dust is attracted directly to the microfiber heads, preventing it from spreading or settling back on surfaces. This ensures a cleaner environment with minimal effort, ideal for homes, offices, or vehicles. The two detachable microfiber heads are also washable and reusable, making this a sustainable and long-lasting solution.

Designed for Flexibility and Convenience

The bendable inner core allows you to navigate around corners, tight spaces, and even the top of shelves or fan blades with ease. Plus, the optional hanging holes make storage simple and hassle-free, keeping your duster organized and ready for use whenever you need it.

Why Choose the iHUFeather Rainbow Static Duster?

  • 24-inch long and durable static duster with detachable handle
  • Two detachable and washable microfiber dusting heads included
  • Bendable inner core allows access to tight and hard-to-reach spaces
  • Hanging holes for easy storage and convenience
  • Perfect for cleaning furniture, car interiors, and more without raising dust

Your Home, Office, or Car Deserves Better

Whether you're deep cleaning your living room, maintaining your vehicle’s interior, or organizing your desk, the iHUFeather Rainbow Static Duster is the tool that fits your needs. It combines functionality with a sleek design to enhance your cleaning experience and keep your home or workspace fresh and clean.
