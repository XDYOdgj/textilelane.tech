MoLKENE Swedish Dishcloths - The Sustainable Cleaning Solution

Looking for an effective and eco-conscious way to clean your kitchen, bathroom, or office? MoLKENE Swedish dishcloths are the perfect alternative to disposable paper towels. These grey cloths are designed with super absorbent cellulose sponge material that can soak up to 20 times their weight, making them ideal for wiping, scrubbing, and even mopping.

Not only are they powerful against dirt and grime, but their non-scratch design ensures they are gentle and safe for all surfaces, including glass and stainless steel. Crafted from a blend of 30% cotton and 70% biodegradable cellulose wood pulp, these dishcloths are fully compostable within 90 days and are kind to the environment. By using one cloth instead of 15 paper towels, you can slash your cleaning costs and reduce your environmental impact significantly.

Key Features

  • Super absorbent, can soak up to 20x their weight
  • 100% biodegradable and compostable in 90 days
  • Non-scratch, safe for delicate surfaces and dishes
  • Reusable and washable for long-term use and savings
  • Quick drying, prevents lingering odors and mildew

Why Choose MoLKENE?

MoLKENE offers a versatile and sustainable cleaning companion that works across multiple environments—kitchens, bathrooms, offices, and even cars. Whether you're cleaning countertops or fixing spills, these cloths are efficient and easy to use. Just toss them in your washer or on the top rack of your dishwasher, and they're ready to go again. Their rapid drying capability means they stay fresh and odor-free, and you won't have to worry about mold forming on the fabric.

With a focus on environmental responsibility, MoLKENE provides a practical solution that helps you reduce single-use towel waste without sacrificing performance or convenience. These eco-friendly sponge towels are more than just a cleaning tool—they're a step towards a greener, cleaner lifestyle.
