Bell+Howell Spin Scrubber

A smart, efficient, and powerful cleaning solution for all your daily needs.

Effortless Cleaning for Every Surface

The Bell+Howell Spin Scrubber is a must-have for anyone who wants to make cleaning faster and easier. Its cordless and rechargeable design means you have full mobility without the hassle of cords. Whether you're tackling bathroom tiles, grout, or even tough-to-reach corners, this tool is built to deliver sparkling results with minimal effort.

Versatile Brush Heads for Every Job

With three different rotating brush heads, the Spin Scrubber is designed to handle a variety of cleaning tasks. It adapts to flat surfaces, deep cracks, and tight spaces, making it perfect for deep cleaning. This versatility ensures that you can clean everything from your shower to your tile floors without switching tools.

Extendable Handle for Tall and Hard-to-Reach Areas

The extendable handle of the Spin Scrubber can reach up to 39 inches, making it ideal for cleaning high areas in the bathroom or even air conditioning vents. This feature saves your back and increases your cleaning efficiency with one simple tool.

High-Speed Rotation for Maximum Cleaning Power

Featuring over 300 RPM, the Spin Scrubber delivers fast and effective deep cleaning. The high-speed rotation ensures that stubborn stains and dirt are removed quickly, leaving behind a clean and fresh surface. This makes it perfect for those who want to get the job done in record time.

Long-Lasting Battery and Improved Torque

Equipped with a longer battery life and increased torque, the Spin Scrubber ensures durability and reliability. It can provide up to 30 minutes of continuous cleaning power, making it ideal for large cleaning projects. This is more than just a tool — it's a serious upgrade for all your cleaning needs.

Why Choose the Spin Scrubber?

Whether you're looking for a cordless scrubber that saves effort or a tile cleaner that works fast, the Bell+Howell Spin Scrubber is the perfect fit.
